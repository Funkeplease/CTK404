Hello, I am at Illinois State University in Normal, Illinois.

I love Illinois State University

Illinois State University is also known as I.S.U. that is its shortform. Funfact, ISU students are called Redbirds. Go Redbirds!

Studting Creative Technologies at ISU is such an wholesome experience!

The department of Creative Technologies is located in Woonsook Kim School of Arts.

Creative Technologies = CTK

CTK is the shortform for Creative Technologies and all Creative Technologies courses start with CTK. E.g CTK 330 is for Web Video and CTK 303 is for Web design and development.

CTK has an amazing portfoli of lecturers and an even more amazing range of courses!

I hope you study CTK at ISU, you'll love it too. I mean myself and a million other people love it so will you!.
